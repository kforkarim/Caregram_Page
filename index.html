Features

Everything the family needs, in one calm place.

Every family member

Separate profiles for you, your kids, and your parents — each with their own records and emergency info.

Smart card scanning

🔒 Private by design

Your records never touch our servers.

Caregram is built so your family's health information stays yours. Everything lives on your device. Scanning and transcription happen on-device. The only copy that ever leaves is the backup to your own iCloud — if you choose to turn it on.

  • ✓On-device by default. Records are stored on your iPhone, not in our cloud.
  • ✓On-device intelligence. Card and bottle scanning and voice transcription run on your phone.
  • ✓Your iCloud, your choice. Optional backup goes to your private iCloud database — never ours.
  • ✓No accounts, no tracking. No sign-up, no ads, no selling data.
